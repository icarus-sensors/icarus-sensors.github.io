Our Mission

We want to challenge status quo in fitness and health device ecosystems.

Open Source & Hardware

We believe in open and friendly world. Everyone should have the same access to technical inventions without any limitations. Open Source and Open Hardware initiatives allow us to make this real.

Community Driven

It requires lot of individual human beings to make something that is really powerful and can change the way we live. We turn to the Community to ask for help with making our dream real. Lets build Icarus system together.

Fully Expandable

Whole idea behind Icarus system come from micro-devices that can interact with each other through the air. Every single of them behave independently and can live on its own. That is why it is so easy to add new pieces.

About

To build system like that it needs time, knowledge and lot of patient.

  • September 2014

    Born of Icarus System

    Everything starts with a simple idea. It grows and become something real. So real that you can almost feel that it is already done. Journey begins with planning, brainstorming, collecting materials and tools.

  • October 2014

    Heartbeat of the system

    With help of nRF51822 chip we were able to get some heartbeat from lifeless silicon to our test phones. It was very important moment for all of us as every single components of Icarus Sensor System will be based on that chip.

  • "December 2014"

    First test ride

    Our first goal is to build fully functional cadence and speed sensor that connect to your phone through Bluetooth Low Energy interface.
    Check out the Icarus Bike Sensor page for more details.
